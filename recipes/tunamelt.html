Tuna melt sandwich

A quick and simple recipe for when craving a tasty sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of preferred bread
  • 1 can of tuna
  • 1 Tbsp of mayo
  • Salt, pepper, or any other preferred seasoning
  • Lemon/lime juice
  • Butter or spray
  • Several pepperoncinis

Steps

  1. Drain tuna can and empty contents into bowl
  2. Add Tbsp of mayo and mix well
  3. Add seasoning and juices
  4. Cut pepperoncinis into small pieces and mix them in
  5. Put pan on stove and set heat to med-low
  6. Butter the two outer sides of the sandwich and place one on the pan
  7. Add tuna mix evenly on top and then place last slice on top
  8. When bread starts to turn golden-brown, flip it and repeat until satisfied
  9. Enjoy!
