Chicken and rice meal

Basic all around meal for consistent gains.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (8oz)
  • Cup of rice
  • Cup of broccoli

Steps

  1. Clean rice
  2. Fill rice cooker with rice, input desired settings, and cook
  3. Wash and cut chicken breast
  4. Season chicken breasts with preferred seasoning
  5. When fetching pan to cook chicken in also set aside a pot of water for the broccoli
  6. Cook chicken in pan on medium-high heat until white and juicy on inside
  7. Start boiling water soon after chicken is cooking to have everything ready at the same time
  8. Put broccoli in water and after it comes to a boil move the pot off the heat and strain the pot
