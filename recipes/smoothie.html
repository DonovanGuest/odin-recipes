Donovan's smoothie

A simple but nutritious smoothie to squeeze in some extra calories.

Ingredients

  • Between 1.5 - 2 cups of water
  • 1 Tbsp of honey
  • 1 banana
  • 3 Tbsp of chia seeds or flaxseed
  • 1 cup of mixed fruit
  • 1/2 cup of oatmeal or hemp hearts
  • 1 scoop of greens or pea protein

Steps

  1. Fill blender with water
  2. Add the remaining ingredients
  3. Blend on high until uniform consistency
  4. Pour into preferred container and enjoy!
